# ZK-ML Weight & Reasoning Verification Pipeline

## 1. Requirement
Institutional AI systems must provide cryptographic proof that the inference turn was executed using a specific, safety-validated weight-set (LoRA adapter) and that the reasoning steps followed the GDL Master Canon.

## 2. Pipeline Architecture
1. **Weight Committal:** Model weights are hashed and the Merkle root is registered in the **OmniSentinel** ledger.
2. **Circuit Generation:** A zk-SNARK circuit (Circom) is generated that takes the weights, prompt, and output as private inputs and verifies:
   - The weights match the registered Merkle root.
   - The output was generated by a valid forward-pass of the transformer kernel.
   - The output does not violate the active GDL invariants.
3. **Proof Emission:** The sidecar emits a Groth16 or Plonky2 proof for every high-value turn.
4. **Relaying:** The **ZK Relayer** aggregates these proofs into batches for gas-efficient committal.

## 3. Tooling
- **Prover:** Plonky2 (for speed) or Starky.
- **Frontend:** Circom 2.1+.
- **Ledger Binding:** Solidity Verifier contracts generated by the SNARK compiler.
