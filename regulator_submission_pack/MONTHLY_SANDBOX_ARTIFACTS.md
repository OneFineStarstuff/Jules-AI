## 3. Best Practices for Regulator Communication
- **State-Linked Reporting:** All monthly summaries MUST be anchored to the current GSM state (STEADY, ELEVATED, etc.).
- **Indelible Citations:** Every claim of compliance MUST cite a specific Merkle STH and ZK-Proof ID.
- **Transparency without Leakage:** Use the Verifier Node CLI to demonstrate technical success rather than presenting raw model trace text.
- **Response SLAs:**
    - Critical Queries: < 4 hours.
    - Standard Queries: < 2 business days.
    - Evidence Requests: < 5 business days.
