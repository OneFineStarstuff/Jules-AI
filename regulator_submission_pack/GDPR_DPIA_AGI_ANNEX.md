# GDPR Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) - Annex: AGI Swarms

## 1. Description of Processing
Processing involves autonomous reasoning agents (G-SIFI Trading and Credit swarms) making high-value financial decisions affecting natural persons.

## 2. Assessment of Necessity and Proportionality
- **Automation:** Proportional to the scale of global liquidity management.
- **Explainability:** Ensured via **WorkflowAI Pro** feature-level contribution scores.
- **PII Protection:** Mandatory Salted SHA-256 hashing at ingestion.

## 3. Risks to Rights and Freedoms
- **Risk:** Autonomous bias in credit scoring.
- **Mitigation:** **Fiduciary Guardrail Engine** validates all credit decisions against bias-mitigation LoRA adapters in the TEE.
- **Risk:** Deceptive alignment of agents.
- **Mitigation:** **Resonance Metrics (CDS)** and **RGPP Probes** perform real-time monitoring of internal hidden states.

## 4. Measures for Mitigation
- **Article 22 Compliance:** Human-in-the-loop (HITL) triggers on the **Governance Dashboard** for any turn where confidence < 0.85.
- **Confidentiality:** Mandatory **AMD SEV-SNP** memory encryption ensures data is never exposed to the host hypervisor.
