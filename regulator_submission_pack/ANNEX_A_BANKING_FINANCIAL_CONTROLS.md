# Annex A: Banking & Financial Integrity Controls
**Regulatory Alignment:** Basel III/IV, SR 11-7, MAS FEAT, SEC 17a-4

## 1. Financial Guardrails
- **LiquidGuard AI Solvers:** Mandatory gating for all high-value transactions.
- **Credit Nexus Monitoring:** Real-time G-SRI calculation for agentic credit expansion.
- **SMCR Accountability:** Mapping of every agentic transaction to a Senior Manager (Human-in-the-Loop).

## 2. PQC WORM Retention
- **Retention Period:** 10 years (SEC 17a-4 compliant).
- **Audit Access:** Read-only REST API with mTLS and ZK-authentication.
