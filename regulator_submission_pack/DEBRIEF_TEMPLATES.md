# Regulator & Internal Debrief Templates

## 1. 24-Hour Regulator Debrief Summary
**To:** Sandbox Office / Supervisory Lead
**Subject:** Post-Demonstration Summary — [Date]
**Content:**
1.  **Summary of Demo:** Brief overview of states and evidence pipelines shown.
2.  **Regulator Queries:** Consolidated list of questions raised during Phase 5.
3.  **Action Items:** Immediate clarifications or additional evidence requested.
4.  **Next Steps:** Date of next monthly checkpoint.

## 2. Internal Post-Demo Debrief
**Participants:** Demo Team, Apex Architect
**Agenda:**
1.  **Technical Performance:** Did any fallbacks trigger? Efficiency of the ZK prover?
2.  **Messaging Efficacy:** Did the "Privacy-preserving verification" message resonate?
3.  **Feedback Capture:** Detailed notes on regulator body language and specific concerns.
4.  **Sandbox Tracker Update:** Log any new requirements or "Sandbox Observations" identified.

## 3. Sandbox Issue Tracker Best Practices
- **Classification:** Categorize issues into "Technical", "Regulatory", or "Protocol".
- **GSM Linkage:** Map every issue to a specific GSM state or GDL rule.
- **Evidence Loop:** Close every issue by attaching a PQC-signed evidence artifact.
