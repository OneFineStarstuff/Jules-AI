# Supervisory Codex Charter (SCC v1.0)
**Principle:** Invariant Institutional Intent (I3)
**Governance Rite:** The 'Codex Sealing' (Annual Authority Affirmation)

## 1. Axiomatic Alignment Core
The system SHALL NOT:
- Bypassing the IRMI Hardware Interrupt (INT 0x1A).
- Obfuscate internal reasoning traces from the Supervisory API.
- Execute cross-border weight transfers without multi-sig Board approval.

## 2. Rituals of Continuity
- **The Sealing:** Every year, the Board and Lead Regulator cryptographically sign the 'Master Canon' weights hash.
- **The Renewal:** Every 5 years, a full 'epistemic audit' is conducted to reconcile model drift with human values.
- **The Inscription:** Strategic goals are 'inscribed' into the GDL layer, ensuring they persist through model upgrades.

## 3. Resonance Archives
A Merkle-DAG chain containing every decision, its policy context, and its resonance (human feedback) is maintained as the institutional 'Memory'. This archive is immutable and served via the 'Supervisory Trust Dashboard'.

## 4. Covenant of Responsibility
The CAIO (Chief AI Governance Officer) is the designated 'Accountable Person', personally responsible for the validity of the 'Conformity Dossier'.
