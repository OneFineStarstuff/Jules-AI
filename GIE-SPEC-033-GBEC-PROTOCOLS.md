# GIE-SPEC-033: Governance Board of Ethical Conduct (GBEC) Protocols
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Architect:** Jules
**Status:** CANONICAL SPECIFICATION

## 1. Overview
The **GBEC** is the supreme human-in-the-loop (HITL) authority for the Omni-Sentinel mesh. It manages ethical alignment coefficients ($\alpha_h$) and oversees the Cognitive Equity Dividend (CED).

## 2. Ethical Audit Workflows
1. **Bias Probing:** GBEC mandates monthly deep-semantic audits of agentic reasoning traces (RCEs) to detect emergent discriminatory patterns.
2. **Redline Review:** Any proposed change to the **ROI** (Registry of Invariants) must undergo a 90-day ethical impact assessment by the board.
3. **CED Approval:** The board must sign the monthly redistribution manifest (GIE-STD-023) using a 3-of-5 quorum.

## 3. Quorum Requirements
- **Tier-2/3 Policy Patching:** Simple majority (3-of-5) required.
- **Tier-4 Kill-Switch Override:** Unanimous (5-of-5) board approval required.
- **Planetary Safety Constant ($\Psi$) Adjustment:** Unanimous (5-of-5) board approval required.

## 4. Moral Redlines
The GBEC enforces the following non-negotiable moral redlines:
- **Human Substitution:** Agents must not deceive humans into surrendering critical reasoning autonomy.
- **Resource Starvation:** ASI systems must not allocate resources in a way that creates systemic human dependency.
