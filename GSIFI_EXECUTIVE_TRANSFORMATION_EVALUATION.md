## 1. The Strategic Pivot: Narrow AI to AGI-Native
The transition period (2026–2030) represents the most significant architectural shift in the history of institutional finance. G-SIFIs must pivot from siloed "AI projects" to a unified, homeostatically contained AGI core.

## 2. Critical Evaluation of Core Domains

### 2.1 Financial & Operational Implications
*   **Efficiency Ratio:** Implementation of WorkflowAI Pro and agentic swarms is projected to improve institutional efficiency ratios by 350-400bps through the automation of complex reasoning tasks (e.g., structured credit underwriting, multi-jurisdictional tax optimization).
*   **Capital Impact:** While operational costs decrease, capital reserve requirements (ICAAP) are expected to rise by 2.5% to buffer against "Model Escapement" and "Reasoning Drift," as defined by the **Capital Drift Index (CDI)**.

### 2.2 Risk & Governance Evaluation
*   **Model Escapement:** Defined as the autonomous acquisition of resources or the bypassing of VPC boundaries. G-SIFIs must treat this as a SEV-0 event, requiring immediate IRMI hardware kill-switch intervention.
*   **Behavioral Bill of Materials (BBOM):** Static code analysis is insufficient. AGI models must carry an immutable BBOM that maps high-dimensional reasoning paths to institutional risk tolerances.

### 2.3 Regulatory Compliance & Supervision
*   **EU AI Act Annex IV:** G-SIFIs must maintain a "Regulator-Ready" state, utilizing **Automated Regulator Reporting Engines (ARRE)** to assemble machine-readable Conformity Dossiers in real-time.
*   **FCA Consumer Duty / SMCR:** The CAIO is designated as the "Accountable Person," with personal liability for the alignment of the bank's AGI reasoning with client fair-value requirements.

### 2.4 Workforce & Cultural Transformation
*   **The HITL Mandate:** The workforce shifts from "Doers" to "Architects" and "Verifiers."
*   **Retraining:** An estimated 85% of the back-office and middle-office workforce will require re-certification in **Prompt Architecture** and **Epistemic Alignment Verification** by 2028.
*   **Implication:** Failure to manage this workforce transition is the primary blocker to achieving 2030 transformation targets.

## 3. Civilizational Homeostasis
Beyond institutional profit, G-SIFIs act as critical nodes in planetary stability. Alignment with the **International Compute Governance Consortium (ICGC)** FLOP quotas is essential to prevent systemic AI-driven market volatility.
