# GIE-SPEC-011: Institutional Strategy & Implementation (GIP, GCRF, GFEF)
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Architect:** Jules
**Status:** CANONICAL SPECIFICATION

## 1. Governance Implementation Plan (GIP)
The **GIP** provides the decadal roadmap for institutional GIE onboarding.
- **Phase I (Hardening):** Substrate lockdown and PQC-WORM integration.
- **Phase II (Scaling):** GIEN mesh participation and ZK-reporting.
- **Phase III (Autonomy):** AGA (Agentic Governance Architecture) and planetary quota compliance.

## 2. Governance Corporate Risk Framework (GCRF)
The **GCRF** aligns GIE telemetry with enterprise-wide risk management (ERM).
- **Control:** G-SRI scores are mapped to traditional "Red/Amber/Green" risk dashboards for executive oversight.
- **Reporting:** Automatic generation of Annual Supervisory Review reports from GIRS data.

## 3. Governance Formal Evaluation Framework (GFEF)
**GFEF** is the methodology for TLA+ based verification of all governance protocols.
- **Invariant:** All GFEF-certified protocols must prove freedom from Byzantine equivocation within the GIEN mesh.
- **Standard:** SIP v3.0 compliance is mandatory for GFEF certification.
